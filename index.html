Welcome to Our School!

Established in Ibadan Nigeria in 2020 by Mrs. Taiwo Adeyemo Ibadan vocational college is a private co-educational school offering vacations for craftsman in the building and construction industry with values based on Christian ethics

OUR MISSION

To provide an opportunity for unemployed youth to gain skill and become self-reliant. To provide training and best practices amongst craftsman of the building and construction industry bridging the gap between professionals and craftsmen. To instill excellence and integrity among students thereby increasing the quality of service in the industry.

VISION

To be a Pacesetter in skill development in the building and construction industry in Nigeria

Courses

Check out the great courses we offer

Painting

Painting as a vocational course is an excellent way for students to pursue their passion for art and turn it into a career.

Brick Laying

Bricklaying is a vocational course that teaches students the skills and techniques required to build structures using bricks. Upon completion, students will have the skills necessary to work in the construction industry as bricklayers or masons.

Plumbing

Plumbing is a vocational course that trains students in the installation, maintenance, and repair of plumbing systems. Upon completion, students will have the skills necessary to work as plumbers, pipefitters, or plumbing technicians.

Tiling

Tiling is a vocational course that teaches students the skills and techniques required to install various types of tiles for floors, walls, and other surfaces. Upon completion, students will have the skills necessary to work in the construction industry as tile installers or setters.
